Frequently Asked Questions

Are Phuc Long products suitable for people on a diet?
Some types of tea and coffee can be customized with different levels of sugar and milk according to your taste, making them suitable for people who are dieting or looking to reduce calorie intake.
Does Phuc Long have a membership or rewards program?
Yes! You can register for a Phuc Long Rewards account to earn reward points, receive birthday offers, and enjoy exclusive vouchers every month.
What is the average delivery time?
Depending on the area, the average delivery time is usually 20–30 minutes. During peak hours, delivery times may vary slightly, but we always strive to deliver your order as quickly as possible.
Where can I order Phuc Long drinks online?
You can place an order directly through ShopeeFood, GrabFood, Baemin, or via Phuc Long Coffee & Tea’s official website for fast and convenient delivery.
